Three Strategic Pillars

Pillar 1: Recovery & Security (CAT Labs)

  • Automated PKM detection in petabytes of seized data
  • Quantum-resistant FHE threshold encryption
  • Zero-trust ESI/CEM handling
  • National Digital Asset Recovery & Security Unit (NDARSU)

Pillar 2: VASP Tax Enforcement (VTEIS)

  • IKNAIO Pathfinder + Automator integrated with iTax
  • Real-time fee flow monitoring
  • Offshore & P2P evasion detection
  • CARF & POCAMLA compliance

Pillar 3: Inter-Agency Coordination

  • Treasury-led governance council
  • Training for 100+ officers (Year 1)
  • FATF grey-list exit support
  • Kenya Digital Asset Academy (Year 3)

3-Year Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Foundation

Pilot deployments, NDARSU launch, 100 officers trained

Outcome: KSh 500M recovered

Year 2: Scale-Up

VTEIS nationwide, 300 VASPs onboarded

Outcome: KSh 5B tax recovery

Year 3: Optimization

DeFi tracing, EAC collaboration, Academy launch

Outcome: KSh 15B cumulative
